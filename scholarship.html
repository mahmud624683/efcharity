Education Foundation Scholarship

We are finding those talented students whose educations are interrupted or cannot be executed due to scarcity of money. The brightest students of any organization (like school, college or universities) cannot fulfill their education or face difficulties due to their family’s poor financial condition. Education Foundation arranges a scholarship named “Medha Britti” for those brilliant students. For your information, it’s a very competitive scholarship. It is requested to all those who belong to well-off families that they not apply for the scholarship. The first step for this scholarship is to attend Education Foundation annual merit test exam. Primarily those are getting good marks in the contest can apply for scholarship. Then Education Foundation team talks with the contestants and their parents and teachers to collect information to select the valuable applicants. Those whom are selected for this scholarship will get monthly 500 to 1000 Tk. for one year. During this year, the Education Foundation team will monitor the activities and results in their schools and Education Foundation’s exams of each selected contestant for lengthening their scholarship for the next year. It also depends on next year’s fund raising for this scholarship to be made available again in the coming year.
