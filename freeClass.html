Education Foundation Free Class

"Education Foundation" is a non-profit organisation. It has many doors to do something good task for helpless people in many ways.The people who are involved with this organisation are trying to give their efforts for creating awareness among rural area people about every good works like as humanitarian works.

1st of all - it creates awareness about importance of Education. Preventing early marriage, distributing foods,clothes, medicine etc in disaster area people.As like as it arrange "Free Class " for creating awareness & curiosity among the students to know the unknown.

Now,what is Education Foundation "Free Class "?

Here,"Free Class" means the class which is taken by mainly the public University students but in exchange of students haven't to pay any fees.This "Free Class " usually occurs at the time of differnt vacation .During vacation "Education Foundation" arranges a Free Class for 15-20 days. It is about 2-3 hours daily.About 15-20 days on Free class we (as teachers) try to complete Their (students) important chapters and give them basic technique on this chapter.In this way they can learn & know how they have to read another chapter or topic.We think the main purpose of the "Free Class" is not only teach them(students) on bookish knowledge but also they can learn from this kinds of free class about Public University & know how can they obtain a seat in public universtiy,by the publician students (teachers) .When a teacher teach them about any topic from their book they must emphasis on those kinds of topic which are the most important & how they can learn it easily.For this reason teacher always try to make easy the important topics by his own way.Thus really Help them & make the class enjoying. This way teachers create curiosity about learning among the students & they(students) feel happy to learn more & more.

Gradually, they must get confidence on themselves about their competitive situation.So "Free Class " is very much helpful for the students.
