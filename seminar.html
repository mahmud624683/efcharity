Education Foundation Seminar

We provide guidelines to students during this seminar.We go to each school and arrange seminar.Those who got admission to public universities become the main speaker of the seminar.The speaker gives a speech of about 20-25 minutes long.He mainly emphasises on future guideline.



Objectives of the seminar

  1. To spread awareness about study among the students.
  2. To make them feel that each of them has the potential to shine in life.
  3. To make them feel that they are not marginalized and Education Foundation is always there for all sorts of support if needed.
  4. To make them realize the fact that itâ€™s not a big deal that they are from rural areas and they are not provided with some extra facilities which the students of urban areas have.Because the syllabus is same for all students throughout the country and the textbooks are same as well.
  5. To assure them that if there is any help needed Education Foundation is always there for them.If they want we will take free classes during our vacation .
  6. To make the teachers realize that they should also give more and more effort for the betterment of the students of their respective schools.
  7. To give the students some tips and tricks about how to study efficiently.
  8. To make the students understand why should they attend Education Foundation merit test exam and how important the exam is .


Feedback from the seminar

The feedback from the seminars held till now is beyond our expectation.It looks like the students have got a strong enthusiasm to study harder.They are eager to participate the Education Foundation merit test exam after listening the speech delivered by the speaker.The seminars let the students and teachers know what Education Foundation is and what plans and initiatives we have for the students.
