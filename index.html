Education Foundation

Education Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by Md Lutfor Rahman , Md Tota Mia, Md Tariqul Islam, and Md Kamal Paul. It was established through taking and giving principles, which we termed as the chain of good works. Over the years, those who got benefitted from Education Foundation are now the active leader in the field level. Enlightenment through education is our goal. The motto of the Education Foundation is "Be a good human, and help to be a good human." We are promoting students from rural and underprivileged areas through this foundation. We arrange yearly merit test exams, provide scholarships to bright but needy students for educational purposes, organize educational seminars, and conduct humanitarian works. Currently, we have 40+ volunteers working at the field level. We cover approximately 20000 students from 71 schools through our several programs.
