Who We Are?

We are promoting rural area students by arranging competitions, giving the scholarship to brilliant but needy students for educational purposes, organizing the seminar and other humanitarian works. Currently, we have 50+ volunteers and 100+ active donors for several ongoing projects. We collected 9000 USD in 2018 for our humanitarian and educational projects. In 2018, nearly 800 students participated in our merit test exam from 31 schools. We are expecting 1500 students in 2019 merit test exam.
